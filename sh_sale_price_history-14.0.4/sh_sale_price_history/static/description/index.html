Product Sale Price History

This module useful to show history of sale price for product, you can also track history of sale price of product for different customers. Easy to find rates you given to that customer in past for that product.

Product Sale Price History With Status

Features

You can see sale price history with status. New

Easy to specify no of items you want to keep as previous history.

Easy to filter records by status of sale order (confirmed, done or both).

Useful to get sales price given in past for that product with customer, sales person, qty, etc important details.

This application fluently work with multiple company.
